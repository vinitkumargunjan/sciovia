# Sciovia

**The path of knowing.**

Sciovia is an independent initiative that helps the research community find its way to the right opportunities — calls for papers, funding, faculty and doctoral positions, conferences, and practical guidance on publishing and research integrity.

We share what is useful, curated, and trustworthy. Nothing more.

*An initiative by Dr. Vinit Kumar Gunjan. Not affiliated with any organization.*

---

## The weekly edition

Each week we publish a short, curated digest for researchers, faculty, reviewers, and students. It is free, non-commercial, and takes a few minutes to read.

- **Calls for Papers** — journals and special issues seeking submissions
- **Funding Opportunities** — grants, fellowships, and calls
- **Positions** — faculty, postdoctoral, and PhD openings
- **Researcher of the Week** — celebrating good work in the community
- **One Practical Tip** — a small, usable idea on publishing, reviewing, or research integrity

## What we stand for

> Empowering researchers through knowledge sharing, collaboration, and opportunities.

We celebrate others' achievements, promote opportunities fairly, respect everyone's time, and never exaggerate. Sciovia grows only by being genuinely useful.

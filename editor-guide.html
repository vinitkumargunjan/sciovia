Section Editor Guide

How to post an opportunity

Welcome, and thank you for helping curate Sciovia. Posting takes about a minute — here's all you need. You only ever work in your own section's tab.

1

Open the sheet

Open the “Sciovia Opportunities” Google Sheet — the one shared to your email (check your inbox for the invitation, and bookmark it). Sign in with the Google account it was shared to.

2

Go to your tab

At the bottom of the sheet you'll see tabs — Conferences & Calls, Funding, Positions, Internships, News & Updates. Click your section's tab. (Please only edit your own.)

3

Add a row

Go to the first empty row and fill in the details across the columns. Only Title, Organization, and Link are essential; the rest help but are optional.

ColumnWhat to put
TitleName of the opportunity
TypeFor Conferences & Calls only — e.g. “Conference · Call for Papers”, “Special Session”, or “Journal Special Issue”. Leave blank for other sections.
OrganizationHost university / journal / body
LocationCity, Country / Online
ModeIn-person / Online / Hybrid
DatesEvent or award dates (free text, e.g. “10–12 Nov 2026”)
DeadlineThe deadline date. Leave blank if it's rolling / ongoing.
LinkThe official page (starts with https://) — required.
Added byYour name (optional)
4

Tick “Published”

In the Published column, click the checkbox so it's ticked ✓. This is what puts it on the website. (Untick it any time to take it down.)

5

Done — check the site

Within about a minute it appears on the Opportunities page under your section. That's it — no saving, no code. The sheet saves automatically.

A few small things

✅ Accuracy first. Please add only genuine opportunities with a working link to the original source — it's what makes Sciovia trustworthy.
🗓 Deadlines pass. When an opportunity's deadline is over, untick “Published” (or delete the row) so the list stays current.
🔁 No pressure, no schedule. Add opportunities whenever you come across good ones. Every listing helps a fellow researcher.
❓ Stuck? Email hello@sciovia.org and we'll help.

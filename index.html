What is Sciovia?

An independent home for research opportunities.

Sciovia is a free, non-commercial initiative that helps the research community — students, faculty, reviewers, and independent scholars — discover trustworthy opportunities and share knowledge. We curate calls for papers, funding, positions, internships, and conferences, celebrate good work, and promote ethical research. No hype, no fees, no affiliation — just genuine service.

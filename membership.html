Membership tiers

Find your standing on the path.

Explorer

Students and newcomers to research.

Navigator

Early-career and independent researchers.

Pathfinder

Faculty, scientists, and practising researchers.

Fellow

Established researchers with a sustained record.

Tiers are self-selected and reflect where you are on your research journey — from Explorer to Fellow. Sciovia is an independent community initiative; membership is a mark of belonging, not a professional accreditation.
